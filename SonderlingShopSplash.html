English Version

Hey! My name is Jean-Paul and I run this shop.
Here you will find new as well as used LEGO pieces.
And everything else related: Instructions, Sets, Minifigs.....

What does the condition NEW mean to me?

The individual LEGO parts come from retail sets that I purchase, separate, and store in my small parts storage.

Attention! Sometimes LEGO packs certain parts poorly, and as a result, they may already have scratches.

*I pack glass parts/front panels back into zip bags and store them separately.

What does the condition USED mean to me?

These LEGO parts come from bulk purchases I make and clean carefully.

  • Parts may show varying signs of use.
  • Yellowed or heavily damaged parts are sorted out.

Shipping

I ship exclusively with Deutsche Post/DHL.

- always with tracking -

I allow myself 1–3 business days for processing, depending on the volume of orders.
However, orders are usually shipped on the same day.

* Shipping takes place after receipt of payment *
